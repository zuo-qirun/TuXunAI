# 第 1 步 – 识别危地马拉

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205462986-49dd756c-4fb2-4205-9bd7-5b33865d6bcb.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205464151-1607e993-3b07-4f46-97a4-f357a21a1640.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

危地马拉使用短车牌，打码模糊时通常会带蓝色调，或者左侧有一点绿色。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205472801-a537c384-6289-40f0-9996-e9694b65f424.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

这辆灰色街景车的车顶行李架和后视镜都可以被看到，危地马拉的所有车辆覆盖均是如此。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205528713-76de07a0-55ee-4240-9dd2-fa53c2f87126.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

危地马拉是一个热带国家，大部被热带雨林覆盖。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205651822-1fbd5388-92a5-4de6-bea6-bfdc926675aa.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_71%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

危地马拉的主体建筑风格为淡彩石屋+生锈金属屋顶。裸白砖房也很常见。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205533488-b149a336-a3f6-45a9-82f7-bdb9657c325c.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

和墨西哥类似，危地马拉的道路线通常为白色外线和单黄色中线。危地马拉的道路线通常比墨西哥的细得多。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698209389071-bc8e4408-7df8-4d87-9266-cf455fc1b375.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_64%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205571914-c7bc2e74-fec3-4d29-8f86-e2339aa1d111.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

和墨西哥一样，危地马拉使用“ALTO”停车标。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205527038-1e328d6c-e5ef-427a-9196-f0a24f174b29.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在危地马拉，您经常会看到涂有粉红色、绿色或两者结合的电线杆。

# 第 2 步 – 区域特定线索

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205565332-97f29265-04f1-4df3-ab8b-8add890f4e90.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_71%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

危地马拉的街景覆盖相当少，特别是在其北部。上图中您可以看到街景覆盖了哪些道路。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205543465-80497dc6-2acd-4e49-ae45-3dac29383eac.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_50%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

这是危地马拉的地形高程图。总的来说该国中部是山区，而南北则相当平坦。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205578681-e71032c6-d6d7-4a9e-b12a-ced365dfc72f.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

危地马拉南部沿海主要为完全平坦的农田。建筑通常比北部平地多。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698209482663-a05f4756-dd76-4b07-a522-77ef535bd5b7.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_76%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205595252-64f8feaf-effc-428b-af49-06be0974a86d.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

危地马拉中部地区一般为山区。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205674467-4d3b3a9a-5afd-4f9f-8137-a9cea2c49962.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

危地马拉北部地区通常相当平坦。然而您通常可以看到一些起伏和远丘，而不像南部一样完全平坦。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698209523574-dd790fc3-052b-43fd-b78f-431c3a0a417c.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_76%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205675479-142b6748-f109-4e23-b27a-9d481cd61df2.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

油棕种植园主要分布在两个地区：西南部和中部山脉的北部。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205674975-bc00b308-bf5e-4c17-9dc9-f4253035946c.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

全绿的电线杆主要分布在该国东南部，但也存在于东部偏北的一条道路上，以及最东北Melchor de Mencos附近的一小段路上。

这些电线杆还有小牌子，可以用来进一步缩小道路（详见下条）。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205676365-c11fa646-22e5-435d-abe7-c491cbd0dd32.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_71%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

大多数绿色电线杆都有一个白牌，顶部的两个字符决定您在哪条路上。上图展示了这些道路对应的字符。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205674311-151b723c-8dff-4a56-8c25-9f6c88d3d12e.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在危地马拉的大部分街景车中，右前方的行李架将朝前。这意味着您可以在前方看到一条线。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698209609143-18ec50b1-1327-4bfe-b9bb-d9c95a118836.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_25%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698209685992-42a96e4d-4e6b-489a-9d82-49fced51e350.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_14%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205691273-50cab245-94a1-4c8b-a885-2fcf70ddf2c8.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

危地马拉东南则正好相反，右前行李架和线朝后。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205692772-7697f10c-b1a9-48e8-8054-fca3dbb22780.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

图示南部和东北部的这些地区也有朝后的右前行李架。区别在于右后行李架缺少黑顶薄盖，而其他区域都是有的。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205693192-f83aea01-d5aa-4313-af92-92c0a30f4a1c.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在这些区域，您经常可以在右后行李架上看到一条蓝绳。

有时可能很难或不能看到，这时看起来就像一个正常的后向行李架。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698209747922-0f1a3df0-5540-4c43-b333-1005df16cf0c.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_32%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698209765122-95d92b3f-8578-4afa-a570-2b009904d0ac.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_32%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205694173-e9cb49b7-3e49-4838-8d17-7e1a1f8addad.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

以2开头的电话号码独见于危地马拉城。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205694596-a9524b17-3fe0-47b7-8813-2db7d0ec2902.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

同理，以6开头的电话号码独见于危地马拉省。

以7开头的电话号码只见于危地马拉省外，以3、4或5开头的电话可以在该国任何地方找到。

# 第 3 步 – 聚焦

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205792131-b28065cd-d74e-4947-bec3-595d8d8cf594.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

阿蒂特兰湖（Lago de Atitlán）是目前山区最大的湖泊，它被群峦和火山环绕。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205720174-12dbbfc5-cd3c-4cd0-b5ae-409c8365c67b.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在该国最北部的 Tikal 以北有一条茂密丛林中的小土路。

译注：在华为地图上找到Tikal最简单的方法是点击圣贝尼托（San Benito）北边的Parque Nacional Tikal两行字正中间，然后直接放大。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205790137-550bec21-7761-4d01-9994-1b57fab66809.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

这些带有两条黑纹的白色小桩可见于克萨尔特南戈（Quetzaltenango）东北的一段1号公路。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205825643-99be2f5d-7966-4f7a-aacf-2c145fd28691.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

位于Las Pozas以南的PET-11号公路可以通过略微起伏的景观以及其完全南北的走向来识别。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205812917-85b1ed59-6d75-44cf-8eae-a86c87fdc25c.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

由埃斯昆特拉（Escuintla）直下海岸的CA9号公路可以通过其混凝土路面、上下行分隔来识别。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205724014-ad39ec3e-779d-4d80-ba80-6f602a420c8e.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在Tikal以西有几座玛雅神庙的徒步拍摄街景。

值得一提的是，虽然危地马拉还有其他徒步拍摄街景以玛雅遗址为特色，但这里是目前最主要的一处，包含最大、最完整的神庙结构。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698210166329-37a53d2c-c6ad-47fc-852b-6578d7ea29b9.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_76%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35193536/1698205781990-44cd94b6-99c5-4fb6-af13-5f0461e9dab8.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

这些写有“electricista”字样的方形白贴纸是危地马拉城独有的。
