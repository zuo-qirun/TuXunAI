### 墨西哥

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833560731-0259ff23-8a1a-4890-9233-13de29226497.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

墨西哥的大多数电线杆呈八边形，并由混凝土制成。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833561613-6f9ba056-42aa-4f94-9ac8-a27e512a85a3.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_20%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

形状像橡子的白色绝缘体可以在墨西哥沿海各州看到，不过目前在米却肯州、科利马州和韦拉克鲁斯州最为常见。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833561988-8bd42645-ad5e-42e9-8ff0-7547e6a3b2e5.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

杆顶不对称且绝缘子呈双层的电线杆是墨西哥城、墨西哥州、伊达尔戈州和莫雷洛斯州独有的。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833562614-670bacbe-af5c-4d1d-949d-f3b865d79950.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

横杆较低的木杆主要出现在锡那罗亚州和索诺拉州。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833563028-cf20266c-4220-49f4-b9d0-f14635e23086.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

这种横杆下方悬挂绝缘体的混凝土杆是尤卡坦半岛独有的。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833563681-77c51901-5a18-4270-8103-24151d6e5e3d.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

而这种“悬挂式”电线杆若是木制的，则出现在下加利福尼亚半岛。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833564160-7608db03-420b-41da-9823-a3dd2df32a58.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在杜兰戈州可以看到这种横杆较低的杆，且横杆的—端到杆子本体之间还有—根支撑梁。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833564712-863d6685-1c7b-43e8-af79-c2130a47386c.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

网状电线杆最常见于圣路易斯波托西州、瓜纳华托州和克雷塔罗州。

### 巴拿马

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833565103-b8aca6be-d4a3-41d2-947d-fc3599e4480f.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833565487-ec686e5e-edd5-4663-b238-7a34323353c3.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_17%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833565866-42a55ff4-3e93-4f56-82f4-9013c26a56ac.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

东部省份通常可见垂直的长条金属牌，有白、黄两色。通常是用黑色带子系在杆子上。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833566440-2e6b2796-166d-432b-bb4d-7954addf5c2f.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

西部省份的许多电线杆上都有水平金属板。然而，如果同时见到东西金属板，则东部优先

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833566781-4640c4a3-8198-4111-bcf4-478d1910a992.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

薄薄的黄色金属板，带显眼的黑色边框，有垂直、水平两种样式，在科隆省最为常见。

## 南美洲

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833570562-b31116f0-c711-484e-bc0d-293b1741b006.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

哥伦比亚有大量的圆柱形和八边形混凝土电线杆分布，电线杆通常涂有独特的黑黄 / 黑橙条纹。相比邻国，深色的电线杆在哥伦比亚比更为常见。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833570972-95980893-2e3b-4770-889f-40679e531c67.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

这些电线杆涂两条黑色条带和中间—条较长的黄色条带，最常见于安蒂奥基亚（Antioquia）省。有时，您也可以在科尔多瓦（Cordoba）省找到它们。

### 巴西

巴西最常见电线杆的底部与梯子很相似。底部整体呈矩形，且有很长的（3-4 段）分段。靠近顶部有小孔。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833586602-08ba8145-7b0a-4df6-a9a6-469dfe983e00.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

类似白蘑菇的绝缘体在巴伊亚州[BA]发现，很少在巴西利亚[DF]发现。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833587130-ce4af732-c1bb-4c44-b178-4bba26d5beb4.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

透明绝缘体主要在东北部的阿拉戈斯州[AL]和塞尔希培州[SE]、以及南部的圣卡塔琳娜州[SC]。更罕见的是，它们可以在巴西利亚[DF]和塞阿拉州[CE]看到。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833587518-909e8a20-062e-4c56-b5b2-1400b9e2a198.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

绝缘体的形状类似于线轴。它们主要分布在里约热内卢州[RJ]和圣埃斯皮里图州[ES]，同时可见于塞阿拉州[CE]。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833588090-fcac5988-8bd8-4f33-b51d-39d36dcf86dd.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

塞阿拉州[CE]的绝缘体有时可能特别长而薄。它们也可能在戈亚斯州[GO]出现，但概率较小。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833588893-b7164b0c-863a-40e6-85b6-6fc8ff7e4ed7.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

带有水平绝缘体的电线杆在帕拉州[PA]非常常见，而在马托格罗索州[MT]则不那么常见。它们在阿克里州[AC]、阿马帕州[AP]、托坎廷斯州[TO]、圣卡塔琳娜州[SC]和南里奥格兰德州[RS]很少见。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833589541-fb650ccd-2e9e-4330-a1d3-1adcabcbd973.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

长灰色绝缘体（不包括水平绝缘体）最常见于帕拉、马托格罗索、托坎廷、巴伊亚、南马托格罗索、巴拉那和圣卡塔琳娜等州。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833589908-fa92d703-fa99-4191-ac59-8dce55dd4dd1.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在马拉尼昂州[MA]，具有这些三叉戟形式的宽绝缘体的杆子很常见。这种绝缘体本身在许多州都很常见，但都不是这样排布的。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833590642-d6dd1845-e375-4740-8b2d-714fd4994987.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

绝缘子呈球形或圆锥形，悬挂在绝缘子棒上方的杆上。它们通常被称为“棒棒糖”绝缘体。它们在北里奥格兰德州、帕拉伊巴州、伯南布哥州非常常见，在塞尔希培州更为罕见。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833591023-b85c0a3b-bf67-44b5-a71e-395a227adf01.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

带有灰色底座的深棕色绝缘体几乎只在圣卡塔琳娜和南里奥格兰德

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833591622-bfc6b40a-d7d6-4830-a817-35fa9f32ed11.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

电线杆顶部的这些球体是棕灶鸟的巢穴。它们在巴西南部很常见。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833592030-00f0153e-23f1-4172-a5ed-a4d55c0a9c4f.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在圣保罗，电线杆上有—块正方形的黄色杆漆，上面有三个字母，后面跟着两个数字，  —条黑线，还有四个数字。其中，三个字母代表圣保罗市，但这里—般不可见。

### 阿根廷

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833592469-fc7a2ff1-7713-4782-9869-fd324ff76801.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_19%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

阿根廷的电线杆的常见款式为混凝土圆杆，上有三根电线交替分布。另—种款式由两根圆杆组成，形似梯子，如图例右图

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833592890-b1e807d2-a71b-4716-8e5b-c36fb9a5ac24.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

木制电线杆在阿根廷相对常见，顶部既可以带横杆，也可以不带。

（在南美洲，木制 A 型电线杆只可能出现在阿根廷。）

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833593654-6938dfda-b971-4c0b-9a98-64e7b85cef6e.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833594098-ac50f7d1-05ac-406f-860b-7e97b0307c7b.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

### 玻利维亚

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833595156-cc895596-6b6d-4551-8a01-46963f223798.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833595871-cff7496e-723a-4a3e-ad43-f413eaf8b6da.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

许多木质电线杆，特别是在玻利维亚东部的街景覆盖区域，都有着明显的弯曲。

### 智利

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833597145-99f640a7-08a3-48e7-b33d-2376b04458fb.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

智利使用两边都有凹痕的方形混凝土杆。它们有点类似于巴西梯子杆，但凹痕中的水平支撑较少。上部通常有小针孔。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773833597678-0c669431-62b8-4276-adf1-ca93a455b851.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)
