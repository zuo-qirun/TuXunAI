# 第一部分：辨别哥斯达黎加

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776051395007-02329f0f-6f28-499a-84ac-0fc435762213.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_23%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

地图、域名、行驶方向、相机代数

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776005268923-5efff21a-ce1e-4b6e-83ac-7bffe44cb8f5.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

哥斯达黎加的四代街景是一辆白色皮卡拍的。在很多地方，这辆车要么全露出来，要么露出一部分，但偶尔也会被完全打码。车头还装有一根天线。

巴拿马的皮卡在驾驶舱后面有一个很显眼的白色金属架；

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52538357/1776005451567-3ba7b08a-ce99-4be9-888c-7316cc5164e4.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_30%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

而且在那里车辆被完全打码的情况更常见。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52538357/1776005469704-ff244f6b-06cf-4d73-8851-ce5d79dd01fd.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_29%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

厄瓜多尔用的皮卡则没有天线。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52538357/1776005481658-f1db1d92-b4d1-400e-ae90-9568fa2a6f8f.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_31%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776005504976-76636fdb-5659-4341-8ccd-a8ca019b0b21.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_17%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

哥斯达黎加使用白色的短牌，配以蓝色文字，在马赛克中呈现出淡淡的蓝色。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776005552235-d8525978-b1a6-47eb-b704-305eb515c361.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

哥斯达黎加大部分地区是郁郁葱葱的热带风光，拥有起伏的丘陵、农业平原，贯穿国土中部的尖耸火山，以及散布各处的茂密雨林。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776005607073-c66b6204-28b7-4d60-98a9-067ebc0a390a.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

哥斯达黎加的建筑风格不像巴拿马那样具有殖民色彩。很少有房子使用正经瓦片屋顶，更常见的是深红色金属板或深红色塑料瓦。建筑物通常具有柔和色调的混凝土墙，多为矮小的单户住宅或盒状的两层建筑。这些建筑通常被围栏和防盗窗严密防护，以至于在许多街道上甚至很难看清建筑的全貌。

# ![3.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2024/png/45179955/1723565442927-5f2ed3cb-56cd-49b8-ac20-238f8402ec21.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_43%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

与大多数拉丁美洲国家一样，西班牙语是哥斯达黎加的官方语言。

巴西说葡萄牙语。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776007539884-46e723d2-9cc9-42fe-9f73-7f7ecf85f06f.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

与墨西哥、巴拿马和危地马拉一样，哥斯达黎加的停车标志上也写着“ALTO”。

巴拿马停车标志的字体更粗。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52538357/1776007697471-1d0a5bf0-3987-4797-963e-1d0459ab05eb.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_14%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

此外，在西语系拉美地区的其他国家，这种标志上写的是“PARE”。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776007720626-f57af0e8-175c-4402-877b-6024b5679ba4.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

哥斯达黎加的标准出租车是红的。

巴拿马的出租车是黄的。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776007732367-eb0ec3fa-37db-4495-a985-60e6559d3807.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

哥斯达黎加使用长条形的银色绝缘子。

巴拿马使用的绝缘子种类很多，其中一些也是长条银色的，此外他们还使用短银色、长黑色、UFO型以及白色蘑菇型绝缘子。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52538357/1776008037079-db13b99f-072f-462d-a655-d5d401db462d.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_9%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52538357/1776008006058-82dfb701-6173-41cd-98d0-5757cabdaf8f.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_9%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52538357/1776008009946-b7e7f21f-9813-4a04-8f39-20db039fff56.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_9%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52538357/1776008019219-74b5dcaf-4712-4bb0-b48c-1734c340852c.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_9%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52538357/1776008063968-12408171-8525-4d4d-8714-e110a3aa50fb.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_9%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

另外，巴西也可能出现长条银色绝缘子。

最后，厄瓜多尔使用的是UFO型和蘑菇型绝缘子。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776007878300-e8bc5187-5578-4e94-99c4-1747fab72424.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

最常见的杆顶样式之一是带有非对称绝缘子的等长横杆，即一侧有一个绝缘子，另一侧有两个。此外，这种横杆在电线杆上的位置比通常情况下要低。

在巴拿马也能见到这种带有低横杆且绝缘子呈“2对1”分布的电线杆。不过，哥斯达黎加的电线杆通常带有支撑杆，而这在巴拿马的电线杆上非常罕见。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776047688319-96825f8d-2a2a-456d-9f17-9883d3fa09fc.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在哥斯达黎加，经常能见到这种在电线杆顶端侧面安装了单个绝缘子的电线杆。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776047735246-e9c1cad0-22a1-4d4d-850f-9b5d3c826c04.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

哥斯达黎加的电线杆路灯顶部会带有蓝点，这与巴拿马、哥伦比亚和巴西等其他拉美国家类似。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776047783883-0862516f-e450-4f89-bbfd-4905eb2f39e0.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

这种弯曲90度，成 L形的细金属横杆也很常见。

这种样式在巴拿马、哥伦比亚和厄瓜多尔也经常能见到。不过，总的来说，巴拿马带有横杆的电线杆没哥斯达黎加那么普遍。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776047864953-cbbac1ce-0527-431c-a756-2a98b56a3164.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

许多路牌的背面都有独特的十字形支架。这种情况在菱形牌子中最为常见，而其他牌子大多用常规安装方式。

哥伦比亚的路牌背面几乎总是有十字支架，但他们使用的是白杆。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776048072342-68708752-7e20-4230-a552-c9f978d7d9da.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

 哥斯达黎加的“路桩”是这种带有三个黑圈的橙色的板子。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776048188907-d9231b92-0223-43a1-936d-b7fead5b2c5e.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

哥斯达黎加的电表是圆的，旁边通常还会配有一个大金属箱。

圆形电表在其他北美国家也能见到，主要是墨西哥。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776048273569-0867f295-8023-4876-8367-c274a496332a.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

 在哥斯达黎加，随处可见带有黑鹰吉祥物的Imperial beer广告。

### 基础设施

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776048575044-c34d623c-5a7a-436e-9fbf-591e349b5358.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

电线杆上有时贴有的黑色或白色标签，上面印有多行字。最上面那一行的数字的第一位通常代表特定的省份。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52538357/1776048666679-0b74a626-6147-43dd-b964-7a6951564686.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_13%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

白款

如需更具体的信息，请参阅由 L’Heureux Traité 制作的[这份研究文档](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1v0JPX6oGBOsDQ8x68jGbO3LpWcwU9UcNn9GV3bcwW1M/edit?slide=id.g3790a800eb2_0_247#slide=id.g3790a800eb2_0_247)。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776048710242-76244d9a-af99-4fd1-a22d-5a44acd8f50e.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在圣何塞及其周边地区，可以见到这种非对称的杆顶结构：电线杆一侧有一个绝缘子，另一侧有两个。

绝缘子全部位于电线杆一侧的全非对称式杆顶在全国各地都能见到。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776048820464-7922c679-1c9c-4209-aa84-62116c350035.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在阿拉胡埃拉中部以及跨越省界的埃雷迪亚省，可以见到由铁丝弯曲成数字形状并固定在电线杆上的附件。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776049048399-c4d72231-1e1f-4951-858e-e25cb9e87a43.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在大都会区，特有的标识是带有黑色字体的白色电线杆铭牌。它们通常使用金属箍固定在电线杆上，不过有时这些金属箍可能会脱落。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52538357/1776049177072-6db46b4d-3e1c-43f0-a0bb-5b0cfa04e7ae.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_10%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776049185256-9ce6e7fb-c082-4961-95df-683cf9bf7850.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

通常在卡塔戈省西部可以见到蓝色的字母和数字，组成 P-XXXXX 格式的代码，有时带有白色背景，有时则没有。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/webp/52538357/1776049381887-9e8946d0-b70d-4fef-94cb-357e84026c9c.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_34%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

这种带有二维码且底部贴有小型黑色标签的电线杆标识，常见于圣何塞南部的科拉利略 Corralillo 周边地区。

### 环境

### 杂项
