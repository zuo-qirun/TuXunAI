### 约旦

三种常见的电线杆：圆形混凝土杆具有斜杠支撑、三个水平 Y 杆顶、或薄金属杆带有三个垂直线圈。

（个人认为：约旦最好的识别方法还是看车）

### 阿联酋

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773763036051-9ba708fe-7ac1-4812-936f-3dc8665bcc1e.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_43%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

深棕色电线杆，杆顶由三个绝缘体组成，对称地连接在水平金属棒上。

### 阿曼

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773763080953-28b3c530-732d-49ba-97db-97a1cb1c76d9.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_29%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

深棕色电线杆，但是木杆上方的金属三角形设计是阿曼独有的，这一点可以与阿联酋进行区分

### 卡塔尔

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773763160670-a0553a5f-c83e-4f38-9722-11b58318f79c.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

卡塔尔使用圆杆，杆顶上有3个绝缘体，其中中间的稍高。横杆下有两个斜向支架。

### 哈萨克斯坦与吉尔吉斯斯坦

### 印度

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773836428823-b7a500c0-ceef-4cac-9f6f-e60d060859e2.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_43%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

印度最常见的电线杆为矩形，混凝土材质，杆顶为三叉戟。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773836472172-8de74f1f-8b81-4f59-9484-936d68545088.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_43%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

混凝土多孔杆是古吉拉特邦以及达曼-迪乌、达德拉-纳加尔哈维利这两个联邦属地特有的。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835339039-822fa4bb-01f5-4eeb-9e8f-3ddb4817da9f.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

有两条凹槽的电线杆，在安得拉邦和特伦甘纳邦很常见。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835339442-f873373e-4e19-4c56-93df-01d6a901618f.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

网状的金属杆是锡金邦独有的。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835339825-65db9924-5d00-4165-b5ff-0eef54476878.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

金属多孔杆在喀拉拉邦很常见。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835340212-b3827332-41b8-4e8c-a3c0-45c195b62f34.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

印度东北部以及喜马拉雅山地区的北阿坎德邦和喜马偕尔邦随处可见这种简单的灰色金属杆

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835340826-dd707dca-b7a8-42da-b444-38f39149381c.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

窗户形状的小型金属杆顶，分布于旁遮普和哈里亚纳邦。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835341198-c758c7f9-1844-41d4-a65e-61def3bb0ee5.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

这种三叉戟杆顶分布在卡纳塔克邦，同时散见于哈里亚纳邦

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835341822-e6c2c1ef-dae0-409e-9417-7784dcd5f8dc.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

曼尼普尔邦经常找到这种全部为金属材质的三叉戟杆顶。三叉戟的顶部偶尔还会出现图中所示的短小横杆。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835342227-aa879417-dcac-4824-9eee-be0b93c5aa31.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

杆顶下方倒三角形杆顶，常见于马哈拉施特拉邦、恰蒂斯加尔邦，同时散见于中央邦。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835342794-9cf5aad2-60e4-474d-b29f-fd0c12223d51.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

中央邦的电线杆顶部下面—点有个像倒 A 形的金属杆。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835343186-b85a20ae-ab97-4a96-b187-277ecb137274.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

类似于倒梯形的连接物，在恰蒂斯加尔邦最常见，梅加拉亚邦、特里普拉邦也比较多见

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835343553-b1340032-ed20-4fd8-a71b-5eaca99a9e5d.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835343995-12ab7794-462e-493a-aa53-dc9d90e808d9.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在西孟加拉邦可以找到各种用两根垂直杆固定在杆上的杆顶，它们形成了—个又高又窄的矩形。

西孟加拉邦还有这种有两个横杆的杆顶，彼此通过两侧细细的垂直支架连接。靠下的横杆两侧通过对角支架连接到电线杆上（如图二）

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835344435-385a03bb-af3c-40ca-9f70-d6c843eeee1d.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在东北部，还有可能发现这种—个规则杆顶、 —个不规则杆顶组合起来的电线杆。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835344853-e048cb41-40a4-4368-a903-50490ea08e9d.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

带“<”形状的电线杆，在梅加拉亚邦很常见，同时散见于阿萨姆邦。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835345479-1ef2fa9b-043f-4a2c-b5a9-7cc70d02d78e.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在阿萨姆邦、梅加拉亚邦、米佐拉姆邦，通常会看到带有两个横杆的电线杆，每个横杆上都有两个绝缘体。

（如果有两个以上的横杆，那么可以缩小到梅加拉亚邦、米佐拉姆邦。）

### 斯里兰卡

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835348771-88bc91a9-c1bd-4280-989e-40dd0426ea91.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

斯里兰卡通常使用混凝土电线杆，有两种电线杆是斯里兰卡常用的：

1.侧面有小型针孔的方形杆。这些针孔仅存在于电线杆的顶部。

2.电线杆下半部分有大型透视方孔

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835349260-4850c53f-0eb3-405b-87ce-8e1be32eb03a.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_11%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

“松果型绝缘子”。在斯里兰卡非常常见。

（千万不要看到松果状绝缘体就盲选斯里兰卡，在有些国家，比如加纳，也会使用这种绝缘体）

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835351368-6ba6ab35-5fa0-4f98-917c-4d362d14ad3c.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

这种杆顶有—个不对称横杆，上方还有三角形附件，下方有支撑杆，在加德满都最常见。

### 泰国

3.杆身顶部有—块较短的金属构件，连接第四根电线，与横杆垂直。（注意，苏拉威西北部的杆顶在此处使用—根平行于横杆，且相较明古鲁省更长的金属条）

4.有时杆顶下方还会带有四个较小的白色绝缘子。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2026/png/52876008/1773835371839-d4485392-551f-4254-b9df-9ae7a4ff2b0b.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

西苏门答腊省有—种特殊的电线杆，表现为：

1. 杆身大多由金属制成（也有较少水泥）。

2. 杆顶大致对称，杆顶下的两根支撑杆呈等腰梯形。即与西加里曼丹杆顶相比，两条支撑杆的斜度更大，并且下面多了—根横着的钢条。但是这根钢条和支撑杆是—体的，而不是像苏拉威西北部杆顶那样相互独立。

3. 大多部分时候，位于中间的绝缘子会往—侧偏—点，所以杆顶并不是绝对对称的。
