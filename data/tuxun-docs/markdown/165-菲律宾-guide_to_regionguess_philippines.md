### 景观和植被 Landscape and Vegetation

#### 棕榈树和其它树 Palms and Trees

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125789860-26f9a6fb-c213-47eb-8e01-b70b6e7d36d6.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

橡胶种植园大多出现在棉兰老岛的西部和南部。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125789895-4ec9357d-cec7-4406-b412-46971b6b70b4.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

这种叶子圆圆的，树干很直很细的棕榈（我无法找到它的名字）在吕宋岛的北海岸和比科尔 Bicol 的北海岸最常见。（译注：应为 Livistona saribus 菲律宾蒲葵，感谢周树人老师）

肿柄菊（Tithonia diversifolia）可以用它叶子的独特形状认出来。它在吕宋岛北边和棉兰老岛这两块的干旱山区很常见。

这个不是菲律宾独有的，全世界热带和亚热带地区都有。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125790092-3b5390b4-a292-4e3e-aa3c-0afc6a480d87.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

翅荚决明 (Senna alata) 在巴拉望比其它岛常见。

它的原产地是南美洲。它并不是菲律宾独有的。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125792271-e55dec76-b6b9-4e1b-bafb-8cc0cff22750.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

海芋在全国都能找到，但是一簇一簇的在巴拉望最常见。

### 基础设施 Infrastructure

#### 护栏和石块 Guardrails and Blocks

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125792242-1e4a20d1-aad7-44e4-b178-405956666d99.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

有黄色柱子的护栏在班乃岛、内格罗斯岛与宿务岛最常见。一般的柱子有两种：比较粗的方形或者比较细的圆形。

这个 meta 慎用，偶尔在其它岛也有这种东西。没涂漆/涂白色的柱子也在这三个岛有。在使用这条 meta 之前，请先寻找其它线索。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125792328-275bd397-1065-42bb-a582-00647e7164f6.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

红蓝两色的方块在卡拉巴松 Calabarzon 的东部最常见。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125792370-e65dda08-1e23-45b8-ab9c-1c449c803f95.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

侧边形状是个梯形，镶嵌着很多鹅卵石的水泥块在卡拉巴松 Calabarzon 的东部最常见。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125792836-483bbed5-c7e5-4e9e-8439-b86262a4b542.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

小方块（宽和高都比其它的小）在民马罗巴 MIMAROPA 最常见，特别是民都洛岛。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125794409-add5e9aa-e1ae-405a-86f7-fa0cd0fb6997.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

边上涂黄的方块几乎是宿务北部独有的。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125794418-6911f3af-190a-4bc8-9839-87dd70af6d9b.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

科迪勒拉行政区 Cordillera Administrative Region 和宿务 Cebu 有黑黄相间的护栏和方块。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125794446-6181bae2-e38c-46da-9448-19c86251eb3c.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

DPWH （公共工程和公路部）在莱特岛的东北部会安装独特的黄色牌子。它们一般在这片的大路上能找到。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125794704-99850a56-11a3-40a9-b08a-5162b65399af.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

形状接近正方形，字体也更细的 DPWH 牌子能在萨马岛的西海岸找到。

Similar to ones in Leyte, more square signs with thinner letters can be found on the western coast of Samar.

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125795032-c07d4784-a7d6-4a97-8cfa-ad48e01728db.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

KCC 是一家主要在科罗纳达尔 Koronadal，桑托斯将军城 General Santos 和三宝颜 Zamboanga 营业的商场。它的广告能在这三个城市周围的大路上找到。广告样式会很不同，但是这个独特的蓝色 Logo 不会变。

哥打巴托 Cotabato 的 KCC 超市即将在 2025 年四月营业，因此，哥打巴托的 2025 覆盖很有可能可以发现 KCC 的广告。

#### 杆子和牌子 Poles and Signs

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/webp/52538357/1761125796663-dc347a95-7355-4635-bdc3-141e9851e6e5.webp?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_85%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

电线杆底部五颜六色的涂漆在吕宋岛人口密集且平坦的地区最常见。

虽然白、黄、黑色涂漆在全国都常见，红，橙，粉，蓝，绿色和其他颜色基本上是吕宋岛独有的。
