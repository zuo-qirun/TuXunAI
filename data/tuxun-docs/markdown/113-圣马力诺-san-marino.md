# 第一部分：辨别圣马力诺

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2024/png/35193536/1722942160512-ab890a53-4afc-4106-abdd-62786aa94614.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2024/png/35193536/1722942196293-ee6730f3-c5f2-4b1b-81c0-1f9fb0a57fb9.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

巨神峰（Monte Titano）非常高大，该国各地几乎都能看见。

认准山上的三座塔，便可完全确定巨神峰。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2024/png/35193536/1722942243884-f8c8f52f-5524-4d2c-93a8-af52a0fb661f.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

圣马力诺的景观主要由平缓的农业小丘和蜿蜒的道路构成。地中海型植被通常较为繁茂。

![](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35076970/1678859286946-57e3a704-9b06-46f3-838f-653648f77530.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_21%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

圣马力诺的车牌为短牌，白底蓝字。

其他欧洲国家通常使用更长的车牌，另有一道或数道蓝色条纹。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2024/png/35193536/1722942354770-8d3975d7-9fb5-4093-a2fe-0cb23a9e8d4a.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

路标、电线杆和路牌样式和意大利总体一致。

很多路牌上都写有附近的意大利城市里米尼（Rimini）的名字。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2024/png/35193536/1722942370456-eb57ac65-c1ce-430c-96b4-1144c92ce170.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

和意大利一样，圣马力诺的大多数电线杆都是圆形混凝土制成，该国也有木杆。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2024/png/35193536/1722942427280-9436137b-1c5c-406c-b04b-f3ede8ed5cc2.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

圣马力诺是全球唯一一个使用黄色底色、深红色箭头诱导标志的国家。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2024/png/35193536/1722942451520-2db8de84-0be6-4f0f-91e7-12d4b1199537.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

​

这种涂成蓝色的斑马线和悬挂的人行横道标志非常别出心裁，是圣马力诺独有的。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2024/png/35193536/1722942527002-8fb5d0e4-6bfb-4ea1-80d7-f6787313ca44.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

​

浅绿色的垃圾箱尤其多见。四代街景中也有很多黑色的。

# 第二部分：辨别区域

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2024/png/35193536/1722942601720-8bb6bbd3-6c58-43db-ae96-6e38466e061a.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

圣马力诺超级大街（Superstrada di San Marino）从边境村庄多加纳（Dogana）一直延伸到圣马利诺市（City of San Marino）。这是该国唯一一条有分隔栏的道路，中间用金属护栏隔开。
