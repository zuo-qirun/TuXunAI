# 第一节 - 辨识挪威

![no_landscape.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306780273-aa8b2226-8f3a-409a-b7f8-645535400abe.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威山峦起伏，峡湾遍布。

芬兰、丹麦地形平坦，瑞典则平地、丘陵、山脉皆具。

![no_architecture.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306786197-5dfb93a7-18ef-4819-9c13-cc147d4c16f8.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威房屋大多为木质。很多都是涂红的（也是典型的瑞典和芬兰房子）。涂黑的也有不少，比瑞典和芬兰多。

虽然房子很像瑞典和芬兰的，但是还是有一些区别。瑞典和芬兰多为红或黄色房屋，挪威有更多白色房屋。挪威的城郊和小镇中心有更多木头房子，相比于瑞典和芬兰来说。瑞典和芬兰的城郊和小镇中心会有更多砖房。

![no_licenceplate.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306788849-08dc90c0-aee2-4384-8ac2-035f24ce74a9.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威车牌符合欧盟规范，长白牌，左有蓝条，而货车常见的商用牌照为绿色。

绿色牌照同样出现在匈牙利的电动汽车上。

![no_roadlines.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306794295-db673d4f-b945-43ec-bc6c-2d7ec14a6ba3.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威的大路外漆白色实线，内涂黄橙虚线；小型道路则漆成白色长虚线。

瑞典仅有白色标线，外虚线较短。芬兰的外侧边线总为实线，道路中线或黄或白。

![no_roadlinepattern.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306797265-c526cfec-b0bf-4fc8-b04f-d046fbb714a1.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

道路边线有时是点状的。

![no_language.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306798738-9cb2b268-0813-482c-a4aa-92d515f354ac.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威语包含特殊字母 Ø、Å 和 Æ。

- 这些特殊字母也在其他北欧语言中使用：

- 丹麦语也使用 Ø、Å 和 Æ。

- 瑞典语也使用 Å，但不使用 Ø 或 Æ。

- 冰岛语也使用 Æ，但不使用 Ø 或 Å。

- 芬兰语也使用 Å。

- 但字母出现频率和具体使用的字母不同：

- 挪威语比瑞典语使用更多的 E，而瑞典语则使用更多的 A。

- 挪威语使用“kk”，而瑞典语使用“ck”。

![no_bollard.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306803404-caa2d3bb-38ad-433d-8d82-73123108fcae.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威使用细长的弯曲矩形路桩，内嵌黑色平行四边形反光板。

![no_snowpole.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306807195-947ca7eb-6b34-48d8-a0ec-817cdea0d3e3.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威雪杆常为橙色，中间贴有极细白色反光条。

瑞典雪杆的反光条更宽、更高，而芬兰雪杆的反光条在顶部。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/52538357/1748153025844-cee0d2b9-801e-4319-b77a-1a50b58ccf52.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_43%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

带有反光片的木质雪杆也有不少。

木质雪杆其它北欧国家也有，但没有反光片，举例：

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/52538357/1748153216390-11e0fca5-27ab-4414-829f-f1e1f77b1685.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_27%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![no_woodenguardrailsupport.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306807294-ee3a01b4-d42e-4c6d-b112-ade7da14ce16.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

护栏常有木质支柱。

这在瑞典或芬兰并不常见。

![no_pedestriansign.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306816643-5989b59c-e129-4a5a-8e2b-abf3427b3d4b.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威的人行道牌子通常标有四条线，另有画五条线的或行人刻画很有细节而且戴帽子的。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/52538357/1748153385672-b38bddc7-8e79-4f20-bcc2-3cdbfa15e17d.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_10%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/52538357/1748153394863-8daf8cc2-fb40-4282-a887-941607f6b8d4.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_18%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

芬兰的是五条线。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/52538357/1748153421066-1ff318e9-11a6-4408-8393-6ee90fd5df0f.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_11%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

瑞典的是四条线，但是瑞典的牌子上的人物刻画更有细节，而且不戴帽子。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/52538357/1748153440867-9bceebf3-6774-464f-9fa6-513b417bf9da.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_17%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![no_directionalsigns.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306840680-8a1f6676-dd8e-46d1-aa8e-c6940844e669.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威是黄底黑框指路标。道路编号在绿色框内表示欧洲高速公路和主要道路，在白色框内表示小型道路。

![no_msign.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306843167-40fe0c57-9e37-4d90-9135-d0b22739c952.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

小型道路常见蓝底白M标识。

瑞典也有类似的标志，但字体明显不同：M的中间点不延伸到字母的底部。

芬兰没有类似的标志。

![no_busstop.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306845589-3191597d-0d23-468f-bf9e-8abf813cc1d2.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威的公交车牌是一个蓝色标志，上面画着白色的公交车。

瑞典没有这样的标志。

![no_sone.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306847718-6f9cceb0-5922-40b2-bd74-d510da518fc9.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

城区有Sone白色标志以标明限速。

![no_streetnames.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306850404-f54196d9-5b1f-4841-917c-a87f8f82e964.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威的街道名称后缀是vei和gate。附加在路名后面时，形式为-veien和-gata/-gate。

瑞典使用väg/vägen和gata/gatan，gata在路名后另书。

丹麦使用vej和gade。

![no_streetsignattachment.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306858698-2f3e29ad-78ae-4ac5-ac0c-a21e1d33c499.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

路名标志一般于标志柱居中。

瑞典路名标志通常附在标志柱或灯柱的侧面。

![no_roadsigns.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306965376-b5756834-3e3c-4563-988f-3f1efc339830.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威交通标志一般是白底红边。

瑞典和芬兰的道路标志是黄底。

![no_constructionsign.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306967758-10c232ce-a556-4e43-91f8-8d52ccc75dec.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

但挪威的道路施工标志是黄底。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/52538357/1748153598024-c60665cb-bfdc-47d8-8316-9f1e6c1cf8a5.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_43%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

大多数小路牌是被四个可见的钉子钉起来的。这四个钉子构成一个竖着的长方形的四个角。城区还有被框子框住的款式。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/52538357/1748153721178-3762b7de-a175-4c9b-a744-f4f3003df28c.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_15%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![no_chevron.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306975087-2f3c1cad-ce72-49c7-b76d-01d849d60585.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威转向标是黑底黄箭。

![no_kmmarker.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306981041-c05d3496-96e0-45e3-874e-741f2b5bfee1.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

此类白色小正方形标志显示以R或F开头的道路编号和最近城镇的距离，沿中等大小的道路常见。

![no_poles.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306984489-bd14a3e5-012e-4065-b220-92dfd862f1ed.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威电线杆通常是木质的，顶部有小型黑色金属帽。

瑞典和芬兰也多。

![no_grassroof.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306989699-0f6b743e-5ce8-47dc-b167-352a3107f0b0.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威传统房屋的屋顶覆盖着草。

瑞典和芬兰较少

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/52538357/1748154122640-f3b57163-2c64-46b6-9788-03c6c94e7e9a.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_43%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威也有一些四代“小相机”覆盖。这种相机比正常四代相机要低一点。这种相机有一个巨大圆形打码，前部有一点突出。这个打码有时候可以是完全透明的，让它比较难以识别。

大部分西欧国家也有小相机。

# 第二节 - 区域猜测

![no_sami.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700306995492-10160244-24f8-4a0c-8447-8d6d9cb034fe.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威北部地区可能有萨米语双语标志。萨米语有字母Á、Đ，连续使用多个元音，在挪威语中少见。

![no_maple.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307081819-87ff4731-09f8-4ebf-85c9-f19f8b729847.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

枫树主要分布在南部，特别是在奥斯陆周围的农田地区。

![no_moss.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307083927-c32ea2a2-95af-4840-ba0b-ccd5ffd86ee0.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

亮色的苔藓常见于利勒哈默和奥普达尔之间的地区。

![no_farmlands.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307086583-4cca0b63-f006-4cb7-b824-0e0d87354fad.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

大片农田主要分布在三个地区：东南、特隆赫姆、和斯塔万格南部。

![no_farmlandsoslo.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307089442-746d16f5-b9c0-4da5-986c-53e4fd5870d0.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

最大的农田集中在东南部，大致在奥斯陆附近。该地区相对平坦，通常可以看到远处起伏不大的森林丘陵，奥斯陆东南更为平坦。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2025/png/52538357/1748154321984-5770c641-47ee-4781-95c5-d0cff513cae3.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_43%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

特隆赫姆周围的农田多丘陵，比东南部更陡峭、更不平整。

![no_farmlandsjæren.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307101256-7dc1e54a-cf2f-4bb7-93fe-ec26a23ff931.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

与东南部类似，斯塔万格南部的农田也很平坦。然而，这些田地看起来更加开阔，常见圆石墙。此外，大多数田地只有草。

![no_fjordsregion.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307105995-83037a52-ccb6-4411-ac17-246a2de84f33.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威海岸线上峡湾广布。在卑尔根和特隆赫姆之间，高山环绕着深邃的峡湾。

![no_lofoten.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307109176-24ae2b4d-0453-4d2b-8d6a-8fb288aa3d60.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在罗弗敦群岛上可以看到独特、陡峭、多岩石的山脉，树木较少。

![no_rthernvegetation.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307119564-6f733925-e496-487a-a766-ebc27d391a5a.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

挪威东北部地形虽起伏而少山峦，有小白桦树和零星的草地。

![no_highland.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307182346-834b1c41-905e-4c10-b9e7-1a6125c8105e.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在西南内陆，大部分地区都是贫瘠的高地，没有树木或灌木，沿途是多岩的山脉。

![no_rockycoast.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307184784-cbfc1ccf-c839-44fe-9d10-35a10de3c644.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

小海岸松和灌木点缀着裸露的岩石，分布在南部海岸。

![no_hammerfestlandscape.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307187511-4b8ac23f-7aa2-4613-b0a2-7a47a11d6784.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

北部沿海可以看到几乎没有其他植被的草地，只有低矮、深远的山丘。

![no_whitehouses.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307200026-f31f4b34-169e-438d-86aa-6a872547b749.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

尽管整个国家都有全白木屋，但它们在南部海岸尤为常见，那里有整村出现的这些木屋。

![no_roadnumbers.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307201944-2098e63a-79bb-428a-ab4b-bdc6a6512036.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_41%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

还有一小部分以“9”开头的公路，从克里斯蒂安桑往北。

以“Fv”开头的地区道路不遵循这种规则。

![no_rustyguardrail.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/34598262/1700307213453-a1589981-ef9d-423c-9d70-cd38b36e541a.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)
