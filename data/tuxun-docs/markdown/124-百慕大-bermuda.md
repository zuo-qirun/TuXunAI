![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35750154/1699027305651-9704bf65-c688-4d61-927a-2724fce3129d.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

百慕大是英国的海外领土，位于加勒比海以北。在国家连胜模式中会被视为英国。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35750154/1699027368996-e3d6df1a-0ccf-4bd7-baef-63a572b9b5ea.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

百慕大的车辆靠左行驶。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35750154/1699027379949-768c0cf6-78c9-4d10-967e-9ab0b5e07af5.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

百慕大的特征是一辆通常可以看到整个前端的黑色街景车。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35750154/1699027394752-002c5aca-5b00-40af-be62-5e9827655d6f.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

百慕大最典型的景观特征是由小型乔木和大量灌木组成的植被，看上去非常茂密，独栋房屋散布在岛屿的各个角落。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35750154/1699027392982-41adab66-a97e-4e9a-b050-896dbc7ecc16.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

百慕大的车牌都是白色长牌，黑色的数字基本位于牌照的正中间。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35750154/1699027421968-99282126-0534-4bfb-9900-c76cf0ff691b.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

百慕大的大多数道路都只有道路中央的一条黄色实线，没有外部道路线。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35750154/1699027433808-655e0b1b-10a1-46d3-b426-707a2b67eef1.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

你偶尔会在百慕大看到这些白色“之”字形的道路线。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35750154/1699027458551-4bdd47af-80fd-41c1-ae44-5f204c25d89f.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_71%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

百慕大的大多数建筑都是单户石屋，墙体颜色柔和，屋顶几乎全白。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35750154/1699027466837-59d865aa-c751-4e66-bcba-131bff2524e6.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在百慕大，棕榈树非常常见。

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35750154/1699027468637-c65f0df5-35c7-401c-95f0-42f2efe8cb51.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_55%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

在百慕大的街景里，大海永远离你不远，海水通常看起来都是明显的浅蓝色。

# 第二步 – 辨别具体区域

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35750154/1699027481177-f88e68f0-c203-4ec7-94c0-5ae02ddd920f.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

哈密尔顿（Hamilton）是百慕大的最大城市。市中心地区因其许多大型、密集的多层建筑而显得与众不同。

# 第三步 – 特定位置技巧

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35750154/1699027492802-411c181e-5cea-4348-9973-b365ca6e1972.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)

![image.png](https://cdn.nlark.com/yuque/0/2023/png/35750154/1699027497038-5b46e770-c141-416c-9a3e-e05fef20e236.png?x-oss-process=image%2Fwatermark%2Ctype_d3F5LW1pY3JvaGVp%2Csize_63%2Ctext_VHV4dW5Eb2M%3D%2Ccolor_FFFFFF%2Cshadow_50%2Ct_80%2Cg_se%2Cx_10%2Cy_10)
